FC Barcelona, commonly known as Barça, is one of the most iconic football clubs in the world. Founded in 1899 in Barcelona, Catalonia – Spain, the club is famous for its motto "Més que un club", which means "More than a club".

The team plays its home matches at the legendary Camp Nou, the largest stadium in Europe with a capacity of over 99,000 spectators. Throughout its history, Barcelona has won numerous domestic and international trophies including the UEFA Champions League, La Liga, and the Copa del Rey.

The club is also known for its famous youth academy, La Masia, which produced legends such as Lionel Messi, Xavi Hernández, and Andrés Iniesta. Barcelona became globally admired for its unique style of play based on possession and quick passing, known as "Tiki-Taka".

Today, FC Barcelona remains a symbol of Catalan identity and a global benchmark for beautiful football, combining its rich history with new generations of stars continuing to write the club’s legacy.
